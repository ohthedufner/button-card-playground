Blank Card — The Invisible Tile
color_type: blank-card · no entity required
Presets
blank-card is a color_type value, not a separate card type. It renders as a transparent, borderless tile — no background, no icon, no text. Its only purpose is to hold a grid position and force the remaining cards into correct alignment. Set aspect_ratio to control how much space the blank occupies.
color_type: blank-card aspect_ratio
aspect_ratio
Width:height of the space held open

        label-card renders as a horizontal strip — icon on the left, name on the right,
        transparent background. It reads as a visual section divider between groups of tiles.
        Unlike other color_type values, color has no effect on a label-card.
        Add tap_action: navigate to make the header row tappable.
