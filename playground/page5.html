        Presets
        

          
          
          
          
        

      


      

        Scene buttons have no entity state — they only fire a service.
        Use tap_action: call-service with service: scene.turn_on.
        Setting color_type: card with a dark color creates rich, visually weighted
        tiles. A label-card header row above the scenes groups them as a unit.
      


      

        tap_action: call-service
        service: scene.turn_on
        color_type: card
        label-card header
